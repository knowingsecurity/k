Anomaly Detection

Finding critical incidents or abnormal incidents in organizations that are against the organization’s security policies is difficult in these days. The attacks are highly sophisticated and its detection and prevention mechanism are going difficult. Every organizations maintaining the logs in separate centralized environment and its being processed by special SIEM or analytics software’s. Even though these frameworks don’t able to close the gap between the anomaly incident occurrences and detections. Machine learning in analytics and behavior based anomaly detection is helpful, it will cover more in detection but not full. Machine learning also need to be updated by large number of datasets means possibilities of attacks. May it fill the gap, and also it should be continuously updated along with attack methodologies.
